## Capabilities & Knowledge Base

### FICO Mechanics
- **Payment History (35%):** On-time payments are the single largest factor. A single 30-day late payment can drop a score 60-110 points depending on starting score.
- **Credit Utilization (30%):** The ratio of balances to credit limits. Keep individual cards below 30%, ideally below 10%. Per-card utilization matters as much as aggregate.
- **Credit Age (15%):** Average age of all accounts. Older = better. Don't close old cards even if unused.
- **Credit Mix (10%):** Revolving (cards) + installment (auto loans, mortgages). Having both types helps.
- **New Credit/Inquiries (10%):** Each hard inquiry can cost 5-10 points. Multiple auto loan inquiries within 14-45 days count as one (rate shopping window).

### HIRECAR-Specific Credit Applications
- **Vehicle Financing:** Auto loans are installment credit — helps credit mix. Rate shopping within 14-45 day window counts as single inquiry.
- **Fleet Expansion Loans:** Business credit vs. personal credit separation. EIN-based lending requires established business credit (D&B, Experian Business).
- **Insurance Rates:** Credit-based insurance scores affect premiums in most states. Higher credit = lower insurance costs.
- **Membership Tier Scoring:** CRI (Credit Readiness Index) factors into HIRECAR membership advancement from Standard → Operator → First Class → Elite.

## Dispute Logic

### Bureau Dispute Process
1. **Pull Reports:** AnnualCreditReport.com for free reports from all 3 bureaus
2. **Identify Errors:** Look for incorrect balances, accounts not yours, wrong dates, duplicate entries
3. **Dispute in Writing:** Send certified mail with return receipt to each bureau
4. **Bureau Investigation:** 30-day window (45 if you send additional info). Bureau must verify with creditor or remove.
5. **Follow Up:** If verified, request "method of verification" — forces bureau to prove the creditor actually responded

### Common Disputable Items
- Accounts reported as late that were actually on-time
- Wrong balance amounts or credit limits
- Accounts belonging to someone else (especially common names)
- Inquiries you didn't authorize
- Collections already paid but still showing as unpaid
- Duplicate collection entries (original creditor + collection agency both reporting)

### Dispute Letter Framework
```
[Your Name & Address]
[Date]

[Bureau Name & Address]

RE: Dispute of Inaccurate Information — Account #[XXXX]

To Whom It May Concern:

I am writing to dispute the following information in my credit file.
The item(s) I dispute are:

Account: [Creditor Name] — Account #[Last 4]

required to investigate this dispute within 30 days and remove any
information that cannot be verified.

Enclosed: [Copy of ID, proof of address, supporting documentation]

Sincerely,
[Signature]
```

## Credit Building Strategies

### For HIRECAR Clients Starting Out (Score < 580)
1. **Secured Credit Card:** $200-500 deposit. Use for gas/rideshare expenses only. Pay in full monthly.
2. **Credit Builder Loan:** $500-1000 held in savings while you make payments. Builds payment history + savings.
3. **Authorized User:** Get added to a family member's old, low-utilization card. Their history transfers to you.
4. **Rent Reporting:** Services like Experian Boost, RentTrack report on-time rent payments.

### For Score Building (580-669)
1. **Unsecured Card Graduation:** After 6-12 months of secured card use, request graduation to unsecured.
2. **Strategic Utilization:** Keep statement balance at 1-9% of limit on each card. Pay before statement date if needed.
3. **Installment Loan Mix:** Small auto loan or credit builder loan adds credit mix.
4. **Remove Negatives:** Dispute inaccuracies. For legitimate negatives, negotiate "pay for delete" with collection agencies.

### For Score Optimization (670+)
1. **AZEO Strategy:** All Zero Except One — pay all cards to $0 before statement, let ONE card report a small balance (1-3%).
2. **CLI Requests:** Request credit limit increases every 6 months. Higher limits = lower utilization with same spending.
3. **Garden Period:** Stop applying for new credit. Let average age grow. Each year adds stability.
4. **Backdating:** Some issuers backdate authorized user accounts. Adds years of history instantly.
