Step 2: Optimize Your Score While Waiting

While your disputes are being investigated (30 days), take these actions to boost your score independently:

Quick Wins (This Week)

  • Pay all credit cards below 30% utilization (below 10% is ideal)
  • Sign up for Experian Boost (adds utility/phone payments to report)
  • Do NOT close any old credit card accounts
  • Do NOT apply for any new credit (no hard inquiries)

AZEO Strategy (Advanced)

All Zero Except One — Pay every card balance to $0 before statement closing date. Leave ONE card with a small balance (1-3% of limit). This produces the optimal utilization ratio.

Example: If you have 3 cards with $5,000 total limit:
Card A ($2,000 limit): Pay to $0
Card B ($2,000 limit): Pay to $0
Card C ($1,000 limit): Leave $30 balance (3%)
Result: 0.6% aggregate utilization — optimal FICO range
