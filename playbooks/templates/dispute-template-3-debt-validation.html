Re: Formal Demand for Debt Validation

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to formally demand that you validate the debt you claim I owe and provide complete documentation proving your authority to collect on this alleged debt. I dispute the validity of this debt and require full substantiation before I will consider payment. This demand is made pursuant to the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA), Section 809.

Legal Authority: Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) Section 809(b) requires that within 30 days of receiving this validation request, a debt collector must provide verification of the debt, including the amount, the name of the original creditor, and documents supporting the collector's authority to collect. Failure to provide this validation means the debt is legally unverifiable.

Details of the Alleged Debt:

Amount Claimed: [Alleged Amount]
Original Creditor: [Original Creditor Name]
Account Number: [Account Number]
Collection Agency: [Your Agency Name]

Validation Required: Under FDCPA Section 809, I demand that you provide the following within 30 days:

1. Verification of Debt: Complete documentation proving that this debt exists and that the amount claimed is accurate.

2. Proof of Authorization: Documentation proving that you are legally authorized to collect this debt on behalf of the original creditor, including assignment documents, purchase agreement, or power of attorney.

3. Payment History: A complete and detailed payment history for this account from its original opening date, including all payments received and dates.

4. Original Creditor Agreement: A copy of the original creditor agreement signed by me, or other documentation supporting the original creditor relationship.

5. Statute of Limitations: The date the debt was originally incurred and confirmation that this debt is not time-barred under [Your State] law.

6. Chain of Title: If this debt has been sold multiple times, complete documentation of all transfers and assignments.

I do not admit to owing this alleged debt and specifically dispute its validity. Until you provide complete validation meeting the FDCPA requirements above, I consider this debt unverified and will not be making any payments on it.

Important Notice: Failure to provide complete validation within 30 days of receipt of this letter means you must cease all collection efforts on this alleged debt. Continued collection efforts after your failure to validate would constitute a violation of the FDCPA.

I request that all future communication regarding this matter be conducted in writing only. Do not contact me by phone or any other means except to confirm receipt of this letter and to provide the validation documentation requested above.

I expect to receive the validation documentation within 30 days of your receipt of this letter. Please provide this information by certified mail to the address below.

How to Send This Letter
1. Print this letter on your letterhead or plain white paper.
2. Sign the letter by hand above your typed name.
3. Make 2-3 copies of the complete letter.
4. Send via Certified Mail with Return Receipt Requested. The certified mail receipt is essential proof that you made a valid demand.
5. Keep the certified mail receipt and return receipt in a safe place.
6. Do not pay any amount until you receive complete validation documentation.
7. Document any contact from the collection agency after sending this letter.
