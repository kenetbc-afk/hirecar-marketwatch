This law recognizes identity theft as a distinct category of consumer protection.

How to Send This Letter
1. Before sending this letter, file a police report with your local police department and obtain the police report number.
2. File an Identity Theft Report with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) at IdentityTheft.gov to receive your official FTC Report Number.
3. Print this letter on your letterhead or plain white paper.
4. Sign the letter by hand above your typed name.
5. Prepare copies of: (1) this letter, (2) your police report, (3) your FTC Identity Theft Report, and (4) any supporting documentation.
6. Send via Certified Mail with Return Receipt Requested. This is required for legal documentation of your fraud claim.
7. Keep all receipts, return receipts, and documentation for your legal file.
