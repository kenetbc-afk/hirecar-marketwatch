How to Send This Letter
1. Print this letter on your letterhead or plain white paper.
2. Sign the letter by hand above your typed name.
3. Make copies of the letter and any supporting documentation (2-3 sets).
4. Send via Certified Mail with Return Receipt Requested. This provides proof of delivery and is essential for legal documentation. Do NOT send via regular mail.
5. Keep the certified mail receipt and return receipt with your records.
6. Keep a copy of the complete letter package for your personal records.
