2. Scope of Services

2.1 What HIRECAR Provides

HIRECAR provides rideshare operators with educational resources and tools designed to help them understand and improve their financial position. Our Services include:

  • Credit Education: The HIRECREDIT Q1 Credit Playbook and subsequent educational materials that teach Members about credit reports, credit scores, dispute rights under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), and strategies for building positive credit.
  • Dispute Letter Templates: Pre-written, customizable letter templates for common credit dispute scenarios. These templates are educational resources that Members may adapt for their own use.
  • Post-Incident Recovery Guidance (PIFR): Educational resources on documenting income loss, navigating insurance claims, and understanding legal options after a rideshare-related incident.
  • Referral Network: Connections to third-party professionals (attorneys, financial advisors, insurance specialists) who may be able to assist with specific situations. HIRECAR does not endorse, guarantee, or take responsibility for any third-party services.
  • Community Access: Membership in the HIRECAR community for peer support, shared experiences, and resource access (available at applicable tier levels).
  • Progress Tracking: Tools and dashboards to help Members monitor their own credit journey and financial recovery progress.

2.2 What HIRECAR Does NOT Provide

HIRECAR does NOT provide and should not be construed as providing:

  • Legal advice. Our templates and educational materials are informational. They are not a substitute for advice from a licensed attorney.
  • Financial advice. We do not provide investment, tax, or financial planning advice. Consult a licensed financial advisor for personalized guidance.
  • Credit repair services. HIRECAR provides education and tools. We do not directly dispute items on your behalf, contact creditors on your behalf, or perform credit repair services as defined under CROA.
  • Guaranteed outcomes. No specific credit score improvement, dispute result, insurance rate reduction, or financial recovery amount is promised or guaranteed.
  • Insurance or claims processing. HIRECAR provides guidance on documentation and processes but does not file claims, negotiate settlements, or represent Members in any legal or insurance proceeding.
