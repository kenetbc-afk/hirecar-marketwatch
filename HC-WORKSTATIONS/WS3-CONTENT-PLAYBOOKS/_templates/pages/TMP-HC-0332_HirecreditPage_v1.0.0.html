Denied for Loans

Bad credit means higher down payments, rejected applications, and predatory terms on the financing you need to keep driving.

High Insurance Rates

Your credit score directly impacts your insurance premiums. A lower score means you pay more every month to stay on the road.

Limited Options

When your credit is damaged, every financial decision gets harder — from renting an apartment to expanding your fleet.

The Process

How HIRECREDIT Works

1

Credit Assessment

We review your credit reports across all three bureaus and identify every item that can be challenged or improved.

2

Dispute Strategy

Using FCRA-based dispute letters, we build a targeted strategy to address inaccuracies, collections, and negative marks.

3

Active Repair

You follow the playbook step by step — sending disputes, tracking responses, and escalating when needed.

4

Score Building

Beyond disputes, you learn to build positive credit with authorized user strategies, secured cards, and smart utilization.
