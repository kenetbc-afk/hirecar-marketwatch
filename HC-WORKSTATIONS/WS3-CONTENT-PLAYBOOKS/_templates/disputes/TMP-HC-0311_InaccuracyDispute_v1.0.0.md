**RE: Formal Dispute of Inaccurate Information — Request for Investigation Under FCRA §611**

To Whom It May Concern:

I am writing to formally dispute inaccurate information appearing on my credit report maintained by your bureau. Under the Fair Credit Reporting Act, Section 611 (15 U.S.C. §1681i), I have the right to dispute any information in my credit file that I believe to be inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable. I am requesting that your bureau investigate the following item(s) and correct or remove any information that cannot be verified.

| Bureau Reporting | [Equifax / Experian / TransUnion] |

**Nature of the Inaccuracy:**

[DESCRIBE THE SPECIFIC INACCURACY IN DETAIL. Examples:
- "This account shows a late payment in March 2025. I have bank records confirming payment was made on time on March 12, 2025."
- "The reported balance of $2,450 is incorrect. The actual balance was $0 as of January 2025 when the account was paid in full."
- "This account shows as 'Open' but was closed by me on [DATE]."
- "The payment history shows 60 days late, but my records show the maximum delinquency was 30 days."]

**Requested Correction:**

[STATE WHAT THE REPORT SHOULD SAY. Examples:
- "Remove the late payment notation for March 2025 and update payment history to reflect on-time payment."
- "Update the balance to $0 and status to Paid in Full / Closed."
- "Remove this item entirely as it cannot be verified."]

**Supporting Documentation Enclosed:**

[LIST ANY DOCUMENTS YOU ARE INCLUDING. Examples:

**Legal Notice:**

Under FCRA §611(a)(1), you are required to conduct a reasonable investigation of the disputed information within 30 days of receiving this dispute. If the investigation does not verify the accuracy of the disputed information, you are required under FCRA §611(a)(5) to promptly delete or modify the item.

Under FCRA §611(a)(7), you are required to provide me with written notice of the results of your investigation, including: (a) a statement that the reinvestigation is complete, (b) the credit report as modified based on the results, (c) notice that I have the right to request a description of the procedure used, and (d) notice that I may add a statement to my file.

I request that you send me an updated copy of my credit report reflecting any corrections made as a result of this investigation.

## Sending Instructions

1. **Print** this letter after filling in all bracketed fields
2. **Attach copies** (never originals) of any supporting documents
3. **Send via USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt Requested** to the bureau address above
4. **Keep** your certified mail receipt and tracking number
5. **Log** this dispute in your HIRECAR Dispute Tracking Table:
   - Date Sent: [date]
   - Tracking Number: [USPS tracking]
   - Response Due: 30 days from delivery date
6. **Set a calendar reminder** for the 30-day response deadline

**Response Timeline:** The bureau must complete its investigation within **30 days** of receiving your dispute. If they request additional information from you, the deadline extends to **45 days**.
