Common Questions

What's bimonthly billing?
Standard and Operator plans bill every two months instead of monthly. This means fewer charges on your statement and a lower effective rate. You get the same continuous access — just billed less frequently.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes. Tier changes take effect at your next billing cycle. Upgrades unlock new bots and features immediately. Downgrades preserve your data — you just lose access to premium features at renewal.
What are HIRE Bots?
HIRE Bots are AI-powered assistants that handle specific tasks across your operation — from enrollment and credit monitoring to payment processing and dispute resolution. Higher tiers unlock more bots covering more of your workflow.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all plans. Try any tier risk-free — if it's not the right fit, contact us within 7 days for a full refund. No questions asked.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards through Stripe, PayPal, Cash App, and even cash payments through our Cash Transaction Protocol. Elite members also have access to crypto payment channels.
