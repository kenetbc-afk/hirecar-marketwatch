Our Story

From Bean
to Community

Ember & Oak started with a simple idea: that great coffee should be an everyday luxury, not an exception. In 2019, we opened our doors in a converted firehouse with a second-hand roaster and a dream.

Today, we source directly from small farms across three continents, roast every batch in-house, and serve each cup with the care it deserves. Our baristas train for months before pulling their first shot for a customer.

But we are more than coffee. We host open-mic nights, latte art throwdowns, and Saturday morning story hours for kids. This is your neighborhood living room, just with better espresso.
