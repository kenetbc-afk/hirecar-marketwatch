Founder

Ken Eckman

Founder & CEO, HIRECAR, LLC

Ken Eckman built HIRECAR from a single observation: when a collision happens in Los Angeles, the car gets fixed — but everything else falls apart. Credit scores drop. Insurance payments lag. Operators lose vehicles and income. Families wait months for resolution.

HIRECAR exists to close that gap. From HIRECREDIT's post-incident credit recovery to SeedXchange's operator funding to MarketWatch's intelligence layer — every product in the HIRECAR network traces back to one principle: the record is the asset, and standards aren't suggestions.
