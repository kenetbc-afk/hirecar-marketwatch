What is PIFR

Your single record from crash to credit fix.

When an incident happens — a collision, a claim, a dispute — the aftermath is overwhelming. Insurance calls, repair shops, rental logistics, collections notices, and a credit score that starts taking hits. Most people navigate this alone, losing documentation, missing deadlines, and leaving money on the table.

PIFR is HIRECAR's proprietary recovery framework. We open a single, structured file on your behalf that tracks every moving part: legal correspondence, repair status, rental coverage, and your credit dispute strategy. Nothing falls through the cracks.

STEP 01

Incident Intake

We open your PIFR file — collecting all incident documentation, insurance info, and current credit standing.

STEP 02

Case Mapping

Our team maps your recovery path: repair routing, rental placement, legal referral if needed, and credit dispute strategy.

STEP 03

Active Recovery

Disputes are filed. Collections are challenged. Credit bureaus receive structured, compliant letters under your name.

STEP 04

Score Rebuild

We monitor results, add positive tradelines where eligible, and track your score progress until targets are met.
