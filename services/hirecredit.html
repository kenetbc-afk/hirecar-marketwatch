How HIRECREDIT Works

A proven 4-step process to rebuild your credit and unlock financial opportunities

1

Credit Assessment

We pull and analyze your credit reports from all three bureaus. You'll get a clear picture of where you stand and what's holding you back.

2

Dispute Strategy

Our team identifies inaccuracies, fraudulent accounts, and items that can be challenged. We develop a personalized dispute plan for your situation.

3

Active Repair

We execute your dispute strategy, systematically challenging inaccurate items with credit bureaus and creditors. You stay informed every step of the way.

4

Score Building

With negative items removed, we guide you in building positive credit history. Tools and strategies for sustainable credit improvement.
